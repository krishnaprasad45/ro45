About Village

Adoor village is located in Kasaragod Tehsil of Kasaragod district in Kerala, India. It is situated 45km away from Kasaragod, which is both district & sub-district headquarter of Adoor village. As per 2009 stats, Delampady is the gram panchayat of Adoor village. The total geographical area of village is 3417 hectares. Adoor has a total population of 15,482 peoples. There are about 3,057 houses in Adoor village. As per 2019 stats, Adoor villages comes under Uduma assembly & Kasaragod parliamentary constituency.
